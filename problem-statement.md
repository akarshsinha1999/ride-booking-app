(Search → Quote → Booking → Completed).

Night (9 PM–6 AM) and trip length (short <5 km, medium 5–15 km, long >15 km),

Q2. Booking Cancellations   
Compute the overall cancellation rate.  
Break down by driver vs rider cancellations, pickup distance buckets and trip distance 
buckets.

and trip length.  
Analyze the relationship between driver ratings and driver cancellations, and provide 
key insights 
Q3. Critical Stage & Root Cause Analysis

Perform clustering on drivers/riders to discover behavioral segments (e.g., high-quote, 
low-booking drivers).  
Analyze weekly time-series trends for conversion and cancellations to detect 
seasonality.
